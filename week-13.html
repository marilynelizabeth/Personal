MUSEUM OF ME

For this week I decided to use The Museum of Me because it is just so fun and heartfelt to watch. This site logs you in to your Facebook account and creates a several minute long video of your friends, highlights; most used words, and connections. The way that they display all of this, coupled by the sweet nostalgic music in the background, makes this a very warm and loving experience. What amazes me the most is that, in under a minute, the site is able to copy all of your Facebook information, photos, and history and compile it into a personalized video. Though this video is several years old, I think it is a hidden treasure of the Internet that needs to gain more attention! I suggest all of you log in and check out your own Museum of Me exhibit today!

PHONEBLOKS

I decided to use this promotional video for a phone concept called Phoneblocks as my cool internet piece. I chose this video because the concept is quite brilliant and revolutionary, which will hopefully take form sometime within the next few years. The concept is essentially a phone that you will never have to throw out for a new model; instead, you replace individual parts that you wish to upgrade. I find that the idea is amazing but the design of the phone seems a bit dated and possibly a little chunky because of its Lego-styled layout. This creation can help me in relation to my own projects because of the creative invention of it all. It helps spark new ideas in relation to the phone application in class, as well as, projects that I will be working on in my Design It Yourself class.
