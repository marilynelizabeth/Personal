THE SCARECROW

My interesting Internet thing for this week is The Scarecrow video from Chipotleâ€™s new game app. Instead of the actual game, I wanted to focus on the animated video that was released last September. The video is a melancholic view of the fast food world using processed foods. In the end the scarecrow discovers a new, organic way, the Chipotle way. The animation is very detailed and beautiful, surprisingly, something I never imagined a fast food place would release. Because of this video I tried their food for the first time and was pleasantly surprised by how good it was. Obviously the video is a mere marketing ploy to get people to eat at their establishment and buy the app, but I really feel that this is a work of art that has a soul to it.
