THE SCARECROW

Tweet map is a site that warps the world in relation to the amount of twitter tweets that are being posted. Usually anything with JavaScript amazes me because I am very much lacking knowledge in that department, but this is very interesting to notice who tweets the most at what time. I loved that the website rewinds back through the last 24 hours and allows you to see the countries warp over a quickened amount of time. This website really lets you get an insight to what people are spending their time doing, and in what parts of the world they are wasting their time in. I appreciate this site but I think it’s a bit eye opening in a sad way. And maybe it’s because of my bias towards Twitter, but I really don’t see the point of the site.
