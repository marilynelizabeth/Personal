PHONEBLOKS

Today I decided to share the blog Greyscalegorilla because it’s an amazing website that features a group who creates tools and tutorials on AE and other programs. They are an active group and resource for training and tools for those who want to delve into animated graphics. I will definitely be using this blog as a resource for my projects because I have already learned so many things from watching a few of their “Intro to AfterEffects” videos. The difference between them and the Lynda tutorials is that, although they do not cover each and every tool and use in the program (because there are so many,) they do show quick tutorials on how to do things that you would probably really want to learn. I would definitely recommend anyone with an interest in motion graphics to take a look at the blog because they also sell a variety of texture and lighting kits to add to your animations!
